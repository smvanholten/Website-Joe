Narrative Data from Investment Banks

This project collects reports from 20 investment banks. Using the narrative data in these reports, we construct a measure of markets’ expectations of shocks to deficits around major elections. More broadly, the reports can be used to measure market expectations around any market-moving event — elections, data releases, or monetary policy announcements.

Primary Reference

Do Deficits Cause Inflation? A High Frequency Narrative Approach (with Stephan Hobler).

Access

The reports must be hand-collected by contacting individual investment banks. For more information about accessing the reports or their potential uses, please contact j.hazell@lse.ac.uk.
