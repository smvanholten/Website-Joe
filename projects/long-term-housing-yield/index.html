Long Term Housing Yield

This project constructs a new measurement of the long-term housing yield from 2003 to the present, exploiting a natural experiment in UK housing — the extension of long-duration leaseholds. The method produces precise, real-time estimates from publicly available data while avoiding standard misspecification concerns.
